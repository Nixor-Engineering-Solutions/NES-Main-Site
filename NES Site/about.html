About Us

Welcome to Nixor Engineering Services, a pioneer of innovative solutions in the Pakistani tech scene. Based in Nixor College - Karachi, NES is a student-run organization of technical experts focused on delivering the most economical, yet efficient solutions to all problems faced by our community. With a rich history of ingenious creations aimed at providing relief via our technical services, NES is renowned in it's field as a reliable and approachable entity, taking on the toughest challenges presented!
